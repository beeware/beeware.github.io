PyCon US 2018 (Sprint)

Lundi, Mai 14, 2018

BeeWare will be running a sprint at PyCon US 2018.

Russell Keith-Magee et Philip James et Katie McLaughlin will be at the sprints, and will be more than willing to help First Time Contributors earn their own shiny Challenge Coin .

Pour plus d'informations, voir le PyCon US 2018 site web

What is a Sprint?

Code sprints are events held normally after a conference where attendees can use the conference venue to work in small groups on various projects. Learn more about how sprints work with the PyCon US 'What is a sprint?' video
