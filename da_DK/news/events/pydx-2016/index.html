PyDX 2016

Lørdag, oktober 1, 2016

PyDX is a community-driven Python workshop in Portland, focusing on ideas, projects, and best practices in need of more attention in an inclusive and welcoming environment. Philip and Swenson will both be in attendance and speaking. Although they won't be speaking on BeeWare, they're keen to talk to anyone who wants to chat!
