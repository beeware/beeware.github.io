PyCaribbean 2017

Sábado, fevereiro 18, 2017

Russell Keith-Magee fará um keynote PyCaribbean 2017, dando uma apresentação chamada "Why Python?".

Russell Keith-Magee takes a look at Python as a language, offering his perspective on why it is successful, where it is lacking, and what challenges face us as a community going into the future.
