PyCon US 2016

Freitag, juni 3, 2016

BeeWare will be running a sprint at PyCon US 2016.

Philip James and Katie McLaughlin and Russell Keith-Magee will be at the sprints, and will be more than willing to help First Time Contributors earn their own shiny Challenge Coin .

For more information, check the PyCon US 2016 website

What is a Sprint?

Code sprints are events held normally after a conference where attendees can use the conference venue to work in small groups on various projects. Learn more about how sprints work with the PyCon US 'What is a sprint?' video
