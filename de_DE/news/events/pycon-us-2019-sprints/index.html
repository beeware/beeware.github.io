PyCon US 2019 (Sprint)

Montag, Mai 6, 2019

BeeWare will be running a sprint at PyCon US 2019.

Russell Keith-Magee, Philip James, Katie McLaughlin, and Dan Yeaw will be at the sprints, and will be more than willing to help First Time Contributors earn their own shiny Challenge Coin.

For more information, check the PyCon US 2019 website.

What is a Sprint?

Code sprints are events held normally after a conference where attendees can use the conference venue to work in small groups on various projects. Learn more about how sprints work with the PyCon US 'What is a sprint?' video
